# Hero Video Placeholder Notes

A Pixabay video page was reviewed as a possible homepage hero video source.

| Source | URL | Observation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pixabay search results | https://pixabay.com/videos/search/curtain/ | Search results mostly showed generic curtain clips, including several stage-curtain or abstract results that do not fit the requested luxury residential mood. |
| Selected detail page | https://pixabay.com/videos/curtain-wind-silence-silent-museum-122355/ | The video is free to use, but the composition is a narrow vertical curtain clip and does not strongly match the desired full-screen luxury living-space background. |

The implementation should therefore treat the hero video as an easily replaceable placeholder while relying on the generated hero image as the primary visual fallback.
