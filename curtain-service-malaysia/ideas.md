# Idea 1 — Tropical Editorial Heritage

**Design Movement:** Contemporary editorial luxury blended with Southeast Asian residential warmth.

**Core Principles:** The design should feel calm, residential, and trust-led. It should present craftsmanship as a refined domestic service rather than a transactional product. It should use generous negative space, layered typographic hierarchy, and softly offset compositions that feel curated instead of mechanical. It should privilege tactile materiality through linen textures, fabric folds, and warm shadow depth.

**Color Philosophy:** The palette should evoke natural fibers, late-afternoon light, and handcrafted interiors. Warm off-white and creamy linen form the atmosphere, terracotta and brown establish authority and warmth, and muted sand tones soften transitions between sections. The emotional intent is to make the brand feel grounded, expensive, and welcoming without appearing glossy or corporate.

**Layout Paradigm:** Use an asymmetric editorial flow with staggered section blocks, offset imagery, and text panels that alternate left-right tension. Avoid rigid centered bands. Let the page unfold like a magazine feature about a home styling studio.

**Signature Elements:** Oversized serif headlines with restrained letterspacing, fabric-inspired curved dividers or soft drape silhouettes, and framed content blocks with subtle linen-grain surfaces.

**Interaction Philosophy:** Interactions should feel quiet and assured. Hover states should resemble premium print finishing, with slight lifts, opacity shifts, and underline reveals rather than flashy effects.

**Animation:** Use slow parallax drift on key visuals, soft upward fade-ins on section entry, image zoom-on-hover that mimics leaning into texture, and smooth filter transitions in the gallery. Motion should remain understated and elegant.

**Typography System:** Pair **Cormorant Garamond** for display headings with **Manrope** for body and interface text. Headings should use large scale changes between hero, section titles, and card titles. Body text should stay clean and readable with slightly expanded line height. Buttons and labels should use Manrope medium in uppercase or small caps styling.

# Idea 2 — Sculpted Artisan Minimalism

**Design Movement:** Warm brutalist minimalism reinterpreted for interior styling and artisan service.

**Core Principles:** The interface should communicate confidence through structural clarity, high contrast between solid blocks and open space, tactile restraint, and deliberate rhythm. It should feel contemporary and premium, but never cold. It should emphasize service credibility, process transparency, and material sophistication.

**Color Philosophy:** The palette should use cream and sand as sculptural planes, with espresso brown for anchoring text and terracotta appearing in deliberate accents rather than broad washes. The emotional goal is to make the brand feel precise, bespoke, and quietly bold.

**Layout Paradigm:** Use strong sectional architecture with stacked panels, horizontal rule systems, cropped imagery, and occasional full-bleed moments. Instead of centered hero content, use an anchored text block with one strong vertical edge and deliberate negative space around it.

**Signature Elements:** Monolithic section headers, thin border framing inspired by fabric sample books, and oversized numeric markers for process steps and gallery categories.

**Interaction Philosophy:** Every interaction should feel intentional and crisp. Controls should respond with tactile state changes, sliding indicators, and controlled easing that reinforce precision.

**Animation:** Use directional wipes, horizontal reveals, and clipped-mask transitions that feel architectural. Cards should shift subtly on hover with shadow compression rather than floaty lift. Navigation state changes should be sharp but smooth.

**Typography System:** Pair **Bodoni Moda** for dramatic headlines with **Public Sans** for utility text and paragraphs. Use strong scale contrast, disciplined spacing, and restrained italic accents only for emphasis or testimonials.
